# Session Templates

Use these parameters as starting points when designing discussion guides. Adjust based on research objectives and client needs.

## Standard Parameters

| Type | Duration | Sections | Questions | Over-recruit |
| ---- | -------- | -------- | --------- | ------------ |
| Consumer IDI | 60-90 min | 5-6 | 25-35 | 20-40% |
| Expert Interview | 45-60 min | 5-6 | 20-25 | 20-25% |
| Focus Group (FGD) | 90-120 min | 4-6 | 35-45 | 30-40% |
| Ethnography | 2-4 hours | 4-5 | 20-30 | 25-50% |

## Session-Specific Guidance

### Consumer IDI (60-90 min)
- **Structure**: Linear flow, one respondent
- **Depth**: Can go deeper on individual experiences
- **Pacing**: 2.5-3 min average per question
- **Stimulus**: Can test 4-6 concepts individually (4-5 min each)

### Expert Interview (45-60 min)
- **Structure**: Semi-structured, allow for expertise tangents
- **Depth**: Industry/professional perspective
- **Pacing**: 2-2.5 min per question
- **Note**: Budget time for expert's unprompted insights

### Focus Group (90-120 min)
- **Structure**: Group dynamics, exercises, stimulus rotation
- **Depth**: Breadth of perspectives, social validation
- **Pacing**: Allow for group discussion (7-8 min per major topic)
- **Stimulus**: 4-6 concepts with individual write + group discussion

### Ethnography (2-4 hours)
- **Structure**: Observation + interview, in-context
- **Depth**: Behavioral capture, environment observation
- **Pacing**: Flexible, follows respondent's natural flow
- **Note**: Fewer questions, more observation protocols

## Duration-to-Section Mapping (FGD)

| Duration | Recommended Sections |
| -------- | -------------------- |
| 90 min | 4-5 |
| 120 min | 5-6 |
| 150 min | 6-7 |

## Over-Recruitment Rationale

Higher over-recruit for:
- **Ethnography**: Higher no-show risk due to time commitment
- **FGD**: Need full groups for dynamics
- **Niche segments**: Harder-to-reach populations

Lower over-recruit for:
- **Expert interviews**: Pre-qualified, committed professionals
- **Consumer IDI**: Individual scheduling flexibility
