# Consumer Behaviors Reference

Extract observable consumer behaviors from qualitative research data.

## Behavior Analysis Framework

**TRIGGER -> BEHAVIOR -> OUTCOME**
- What prompts the action? -> What do they do? -> What result?

**STATED vs. ACTUAL**
- What they say they do vs. evidence of what they actually do
- "I always..." vs. "but sometimes..."

**WORKAROUNDS & HACKS**
- How do consumers adapt products/services?
- What unofficial behaviors reveal unmet needs?

## What to Flag

- Action verbs: "I check...", "I buy...", "I avoid...", "I compare..."
- Sequences and routines
- Frequency markers: daily, weekly, occasionally
- Triggers that prompt behavior
- Context: where, when, circumstances

## For Each Behavior, Capture

1. **The behavior**: What specifically do they do?
2. **Trigger**: What prompts this action?
3. **Frequency**: How often?
4. **Context**: Where, when, with whom?
5. **Outcome**: What result do they get?
6. **Gap** (if any): Difference between stated and actual behavior

## Behavior Types

| Type | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Routine** | Regular, habitual actions | Daily coffee ritual |
| **Triggered** | Conditional on specific event | Price check when >$50 |
| **Workaround** | Adaptations to solve problems | Use competitor app for X feature |
| **Avoidance** | What they deliberately don't do | Never read terms |
| **Aspirational** | What they want to do but don't | Exercise plans |

## Quality Benchmarks

**Weak Behavior (avoid)**:
- "Consumers value sustainability and want to make environmentally conscious purchases"
  - This is an attitude, not a behavior. No observable action.

**Strong Behavior (aim for)**:
- "Consumers check product recycling symbols before purchase but discard packaging without separating materials"
  - Two specific actions, shows behavior-attitude gap

**Weak Behavior (avoid)**:
- "Consumers sometimes use mobile apps when shopping"
  - Too vague: "sometimes", "various", no trigger

**Strong Behavior (aim for)**:
- "Grocery shoppers scan competitor prices via app before checkout; price gaps exceeding $5 trigger store-switching"
  - Specific context, exact behavior, quantified trigger, outcome
