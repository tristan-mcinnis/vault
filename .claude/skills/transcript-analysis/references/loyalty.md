# Brand Loyalty Reference

Extract factors that drive or hinder brand loyalty from qualitative research data.

## Loyalty Analysis Framework

**BEHAVIORAL vs. ATTITUDINAL vs. EMOTIONAL**
- Behavioral: Repeat purchase despite alternatives
- Attitudinal: Strong preference declared
- Emotional: Identity connection beyond product

**LOYALTY vs. INERTIA**
- True loyalty: Would resist competitive offers
- Inertia: Repeat from habit, would switch easily

## What to Flag

- Loyalty language: "I'll never switch", "Always my first choice", "Part of who I am"
- Switching triggers: What would make them leave
- Advocacy behaviors: Unprompted recommendations
- Stickiness factors: What keeps them locked in

## Loyalty Types

| Type | Definition | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Behavioral** | Consistent repeat purchase | Buys same brand weekly |
| **Attitudinal** | Strong declared preference | "It's the only one I consider" |
| **Emotional** | Identity-based attachment | "This brand reflects who I am" |
| **Inertia** | Passive habit | "I just keep buying it" |

## Loyalty Drivers

**RETENTION FACTORS** (What keeps them)
- Unique product benefits
- Emotional connection or identity alignment
- Accumulated value (points, data, ecosystem)
- Trust built through consistent experiences
- Community or social belonging

**STICKINESS FACTORS** (What prevents switching)
- Switching costs (time, money, effort)
- Ecosystem lock-in
- Learning investment
- Relationship value

**ADVOCACY INDICATORS** (What creates promoters)
- Unprompted recommendations
- Defense of brand against criticism
- Social sharing
- Identity expression through brand

## Defection Triggers

**RELATIONSHIP BREAKERS**
- Quality inconsistency or decline
- Values misalignment or betrayal
- Poor service recovery
- Feeling taken for granted

**SWITCHING CATALYSTS**
- Better alternative emerges
- Life stage changes
- Price threshold exceeded
- Negative publicity

## Quality Benchmarks

**Weak Loyalty Insight (avoid)**:
- "Customers are loyal because they value quality and trust the products"
  - Generic, no mechanism identified, confuses satisfaction with loyalty

**Strong Loyalty Insight (aim for)**:
- "Loyalty stems from personalized barista recognition; relationship continuity prevents switching despite competitor proximity"
  - Specific driver, type identified, switching barrier clear
