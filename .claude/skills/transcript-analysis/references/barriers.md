# Barriers to Adoption Reference

Extract obstacles that prevent consumers from trying, buying, or using products/services from qualitative research data.

## Barriers Analysis Framework

**STATED vs. ACTUAL BARRIERS**
- What people say stops them vs. what actually does
- "I don't have time" often means "not a priority"

**BARRIER SEVERITY**
- Critical blockers (dealbreakers)
- Major barriers (significant resistance)
- Moderate barriers (meaningful friction)
- Minor barriers (speed bumps)

## What to Flag

- Barrier language: "I would, but...", "The problem is...", "If only..."
- Risk language: "I'm worried that..."
- Threshold language: "It's too [expensive/complicated/time-consuming]"
- Access language: "I can't [get/find/reach]..."

## Barrier Categories

| Category | Definition | Example |
|----------|------------|---------|
| **Awareness** | Don't know it exists | "I didn't know this was a thing" |
| **Understanding** | Don't grasp how it works | "I don't get what this does" |
| **Access** | Can't obtain product | "Not available where I live" |
| **Economic** | Price threshold/budget | "Too expensive" |
| **Psychological** | Fear, anxiety, complexity | "Seems too complicated" |
| **Social** | Judgment, privacy fears | "What will people think?" |
| **Technical** | Setup, learning curve | "Wouldn't know how to use it" |
| **Temporal** | Time investment required | "Don't have time for this" |

## Barrier Severity Framework

| Severity | Impact | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **Critical** | Complete prevention | "I'm allergic to main ingredient" |
| **Major** | Strong deterrent | "Setup takes 3 hours with tech help" |
| **Moderate** | Creates hesitation | "Not sure it works for my situation" |
| **Minor** | Slows but doesn't stop | "Need to remember to sign up" |

## Quality Benchmarks

**Weak Barrier (avoid)**:
- "Consumers are hesitant because it seems complicated and expensive"
  - Vague, combines barriers, not actionable

**Strong Barrier (aim for)**:
- "Smart thermostat installation requires electrical rewiring; renters can't modify wiring without landlord permission"
  - Specific requirement, clear affected population, structural barrier

**Weak Barrier (avoid)**:
- "Price is a major barrier preventing most consumers"
  - Assumes without evidence, unsubstantiated

**Strong Barrier (aim for)**:
- "Monthly subscription at $49 exceeds discretionary budget; annual commitment required prevents trial"
  - Specific price, budget context, compounds barrier
