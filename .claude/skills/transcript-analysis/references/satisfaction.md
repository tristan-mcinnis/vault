# Satisfaction & Dissatisfaction Reference

Extract what creates emotional highs and lows in consumer experiences from qualitative research data.

## Satisfaction Analysis Framework

**EXPECTATION → EXPERIENCE → GAP**
- What was expected?
- What was experienced?
- What's the emotional response to the gap?

## What to Flag

- Delight language: "I love...", "It made me feel...", "I'm so impressed..."
- Frustration language: "I hate...", "So disappointed...", "I can't believe..."
- Expectation comparisons: "I expected... but..."
- Consequence statements: "I'll never... again" / "I'm telling everyone..."

## Satisfaction Spectrum

| Level | Definition | Example |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Delight** | Exceeds expectations, memorable | "Magical experience, unexpected surprise" |
| **Satisfaction** | Meets expectations reliably | "Works as promised, no complaints" |
| **Neutrality** | Neither positive nor negative | "It's fine, does the job" |
| **Dissatisfaction** | Falls short, causes frustration | "Didn't live up to promises" |
| **Betrayal** | Violates trust, triggers rejection | "I feel deceived, never again" |

## Satisfaction Drivers

**FUNCTIONAL**: Does it do what it should?
**EMOTIONAL**: Does it make me feel good about myself/choices?
**SOCIAL**: Does it affect how others see me?
**IDENTITY**: Does it align with who I am/want to be?

## Quality Benchmarks

**Weak Factor (avoid)**:
- "Customers appreciate products that work well and deliver good performance"
  - Generic, obvious, no emotional resonance

**Strong Satisfaction (aim for)**:
- "Overnight shipping transforms anxious gift-buying into confident generosity; reliability creates stress-free last-minute shopping"
  - Specific feature, emotional transformation, identity connection

**Weak Factor (avoid)**:
- "Some users mentioned being unhappy with certain aspects"
  - Vague, no specific trigger or emotional detail

**Strong Dissatisfaction (aim for)**:
- "Auto-renewal without warning triggers betrayal; customers feel deceived discovering charges they never authorized"
  - Specific trigger, strong emotion, trust violation
