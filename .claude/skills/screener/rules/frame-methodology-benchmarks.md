# Methodology Benchmarks

Use these benchmarks when scoping screener length and recruitment targets.

## Benchmarks by Methodology

| Methodology | Questions | Completion Time | Over-Recruit |
|-------------|-----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Focus Group (FGD) | 30-45 | 18-22 min | 30-40% |
| In-Depth Interview (IDI) | 15-25 | 10-15 min | 20-40% |
| Ethnography | 15-20 | 10-12 min | 25-50% |
| Online Community | 12-20 | 8-12 min | 50% |
| Expert / B2B | 10-18 | 8-12 min | 20-25% |

## Usage Notes

- **Over-recruit** accounts for no-shows and last-minute dropouts
- **Expert/B2B** screeners are shorter because recruitment pool is smaller
- **Ethnography** needs higher over-recruit due to scheduling complexity
- **Online community** needs highest over-recruit due to lower commitment

## Calculation Example

For a 6-group FGD with 7 participants per group:
- Target: 6 × 7 = 42 participants
- Over-recruit 35%: 42 × 1.35 = 57 recruits needed
