# Insight Levels Framework

Insights progress through three levels of depth. Final deliverables require L3 insights with strategic tradeoffs.

## Levels

| Level | Focus | Example |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **L1 (What)** | Surface observation | "Users want personalization" |
| **L2 (Why)** | Root cause | "Users want personalization because products feel generic" |
| **L3 (So What)** | Strategic imperative | "DO: Build modular customization. DON'T: Offer pre-set variants. BECAUSE: Mass customization drives 20% price premium." |

## Elevation Process

1. **L1 → L2**: Ask "Why does this happen?" until you reach root cause
2. **L2 → L3**: Apply [Strategic Choice Frame](frame-strategic-choice.md) to articulate tradeoff

## Quality Check

L3 insights must:
- Identify what we WILL do
- Identify what we WON'T do
- Explain WHY that tradeoff makes sense
- Quantify impact where possible

## Avoid

| Pattern | Problem |
|---------|---------|
| Stopping at L1 | No actionability |
| L2 without root cause | Still descriptive, not prescriptive |
| L3 without tradeoff | Not a real strategic choice |
